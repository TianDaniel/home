Trophies


Although Tottenham Hotspurs have deem to be a "trophyless club" in recent times, the club has garnered over 26 major trophies, with 8 FA cups, and 2 UEFA cups. The Lilywhites' last trophy came in the 2007-2008 campaign when the won the FA cup. Since them, the team has gone through a trophy drought, no considering exibition trophies.
