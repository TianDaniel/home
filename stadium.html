Tottenham Hotspur Stadium


Tottenham Hotspur Stadium was opened in 2019, replacing the previous stadium White Hart Lane. The state of the art stadium costed over 1 billion pounds and has a capacity of 62,850. The stadium is the home soil of Tottenham Hotspurs, however, it is also used for ocassional NFL matches.
